Instructions

These clues and pictures gudie you on a exploration of nine locations on the Mount Vernon estate. In the upper right corner you will find a map to help you locate your way.

At each location read the clue and look around carefully. You may also want to read the green signs. Enter your answer in the blank spaces. One letter in each word will be in a box. After you have visited all nine locations, use these boxed letters to solve the word puzzle about George Washington.

After you've completed the word puzzle, have your answers checked at the guest services desk in the lobby of the Donald W. Reynolds Education Center and Museum. If your answer to the word puzzle is correct you will recieve a special prize!
